JAY TURSER JT-220-BK

Jay Turser JT-220-BK is an electric guitar from the Jay Turser company.

It has a vintage design with a single-cut body shape based on Les Paul. The case is completely made of mahogany and has a contoured top. The case coating is glossy. The neck is also made of mahogany and glued into the body, which provides the instrument with an additional sustain. The fingerboard is made of rosewood, has 22 frets and inlaid in the form of trapezoids. The deflection of the neck can be adjusted using an anchor.

As electronics, two "humbucker" type pickups are used here, providing a dense saturated sound, 2 volume knobs, 2 tone knobs and a three-position sensor switch.

The guitar fittings are chrome. The pegs are cast.

Features:

body based on Les Paul with a single cutout; case material - mahogany; contour top; glossy finish; glued mahogany neck; rosewood fingerboard, 22 frets, trapezoid inlay; anchor for adjusting the neck; humbuckers in bridge and neck;

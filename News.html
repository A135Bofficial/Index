Kurt Cobain's favorite electric guitar was sold for $4.5 million.

On Sunday, May 22, Kurt Cobain's favorite electric guitar was sold at Julien's auction in New York. With an estimate of $600-800 thousand, the final cost of the instrument was $4.5 million. The guitar was owned by the musician's family.
